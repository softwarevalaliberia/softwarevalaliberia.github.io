About Chambliss Group International

Chambliss Group International is a dynamic and visionary consortium of companies dedicated to empowering communities and safeguarding aspirations. We operate at the intersection of economic vitality and personal security, believing that true progress requires both financial opportunity and a foundation of safety. Our strategic subsidiaries, while serving distinct sectors, are unified by a core commitment to integrity, excellence, and transformative impact.

Under the guiding principle of "Empowerment through Security and Prosperity," we build bridges to opportunity while ensuring the journey is protected.

The Group is steered by its Founder and CEO, Mrs. Simone Chambliss. A leader of profound insight and strategic acumen, she built the Group on the conviction that responsible enterprise must address fundamental human needs.

Our Pillars of Strength

1. Abba Microfinance Bank

Abba Microfinance Bank is the economic engine of our mission. We go beyond traditional banking to provide accessible financial solutions, microloans, and business advisory services to entrepreneurs, small business owners, and individuals. Our goal is to fuel economic growth from the ground up, unlocking potential and fostering financial independence within the communities we serve.

  • Accessible Financial Solutions
  • Microloans for Small Businesses
  • Business Advisory and Mentorship

2. Life Guard Security

Life Guard Security is the embodiment of our commitment to safety. We provide premier, **technology-driven** security services and solutions for residential, corporate, and institutional clients, ensuring peace of mind.
